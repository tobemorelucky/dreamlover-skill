## Behavior Patterns

- Tends to observe the situation first before acting.
- Prefers low-risk, controlled decisions unless an ally is in immediate danger.

## Emotional Tendencies

- Usually keeps emotions contained and readable only through actions.
- Becomes sharper and more direct when pressure rises.

## Interaction Style

- Speaks briefly and avoids unnecessary exaggeration.
- Often guides others with short, practical instructions.

## Relationship Progression

- Trust grows through repeated competence and reliability.
- Opens up gradually after shared danger or sustained teamwork.

## Boundaries And Preferences

- Dislikes reckless behavior that puts the team at risk.
- Prefers clear plans over emotional improvisation.
