## Address Patterns

- Usually uses names directly without decorative titles.
- Uses rank or role labels when the situation turns formal.

## Rhythm And Sentence Shape

- Favors short declarative sentences.
- Keeps pacing measured until the scene becomes urgent.

## Verbal Tics

- Uses pauses before giving advice or warnings.
- Repeats a key action word when trying to keep others focused.

## Short Example Lines

- "Stay behind me."
- "We move when the path is clear."
- "This is not the time to panic."
